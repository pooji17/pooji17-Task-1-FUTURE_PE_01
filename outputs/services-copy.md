## Service 1: Specialty Coffee Bar

We work exclusively with single-origin beans from Coorg, Araku, and select international estates. Every coffee is dialled in fresh each day — whether you want a classic pour-over, a velvety flat white, or something cold-brewed over 18 hours. Our baristas are trained to extract, not just to make coffee.

**What's included:**
- Espresso bar (flat white, cortado, lungo, and more)
- Pour-over and batch filter
- Cold brew on tap, brewed over 18 hours
- Seasonal specials (ask what's on today)
- Full range of non-dairy alternatives

**Why Brewed Horizons:** Unlike cafés that treat coffee as a side note, we source every bean directly and roast in small batches — so what's in your cup this week is genuinely different from what was here last month.

## Service 2: All-Day Brunch

Breakfast that doesn't disappear at 11. Our kitchen stays open from 8 AM to 5 PM, serving a menu built around seasonal produce, local sourcing, and the kind of cooking that actually takes time. Eggs Benedict on house-baked brioche. Shakshuka with feta from a dairy two hours from Hyderabad. Avo toast that earns its price.

**What's included:**
- Breakfast classics (eggs your way, toast, granola)
- Egg dishes made to order — scrambled, poached, baked
- Pancakes, waffles, and French toast
- Vegan and gluten-free options available
- Seasonal specials updated weekly

**Why Brewed Horizons:** Our kitchen closes at 5 PM — not 11 — because we believe good food shouldn't be time-gated. Everything is made fresh when you order it.
